PECAN+ CTF

PECAN+ is a national Capture The Flag (CTF) style cyber security competition for Australian high school-aged competitors (in years 10-12). PECAN+ CTF is led by Edith Cowan University's School of Science as a collaborative initiative that brings together higher education institutions, cyber security industry, government, teachers and students around the aim of raising cyber security awareness, skills and aspirations.

Our 2025 event is now finished. Watch out for details of our 2026 competition.

Fast Facts

  • PECAN+ is a Capture The Flag style of competition - compete to solve cyber security challenges and correctly uncover a digital "flag" (string of text).
  • Challenge categories include cryptography, OSINT, reverse engineering, steganography, exploitation and more.
  • PECAN+ tests problem solving and teamwork, as well as cyber security and computing skills. Challenges range from Beginner through to Advanced.
  • PECAN+ CTF is a free event. Registration includes access to Training Day offerings as well as Competition Day.

  • National awards and prizes for Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced divisions, as well as Top All Female teams.
  • PECAN+ CTF is proudly founded and led by Edith Cowan University in collaboration with partner organisations around Australia.
